INTELLIGENCE AGENCY

The Intelligence Agency (IA) of Site Aether operates in the shadows to preserve the light. Tasked with the collection, analysis, and distribution of sensitive information, we are the Foundation's first line of defense against internal subversion and external threats.

Mission Profile

The Intelligence Agency is responsible for information gathering and espionage activities for the Foundation. Unlike the Department of Security, which focuses on physical protection, the IA focuses on data integrity, counter-espionage, and risk assessment.

Our agents are recruited from top-tier military intelligence, federal bureaus, and anomalous response teams. We operate autonomously, answering directly to Site Command and the O5 Council. We see everything. We hear everything.

Agency Divisions

Foreign Intelligence FID-01
Handles external intelligence collection on Groups of Interest, foreign anomalous activity, and non-Foundation entities. Conducts long-term infiltration and intelligence gathering outside Foundation control zones.
Counterintelligence CID-02
Protects the Foundation from espionage, internal leaks, and hostile infiltration. Monitors personnel, investigates breaches, and neutralizes intelligence threats from within.
Human Intelligence HID-03
Manages informants, undercover assets, and intelligence officers in the field. Focuses on person-to-person intelligence collection, recruitment, and asset handling.
Anomalous Intelligence AID-04
Specializes in intelligence involving anomalous, pretechnological, and memetic phenomena. Collects and analyzes non-conventional intelligence threats and assets.
Covert Operations COD-05
Executes deniable intelligence actions in support of Foundation objectives. Conducts extractions, sabotage, and direct action when intelligence operations require force.
Records & Security (RAISA) RIS-06
Controls classification, storage, and access to intelligence records. Prevents data leaks, manages redactions, and enforces information security standards.
Internal Prosecution OIP-07
Responsible for prosecuting internal Foundation violations, intelligence crimes, and high-clearance misconduct. Works with Internal Security and Intelligence to pursue charges, conduct hearings, and enforce Foundation law.

Guidelines & Conduct

  • PROTOCOL 01: INFORMATION COMPARTMENTALIZATION
    Intelligence is disseminated on a strictly need-to-know basis. Sharing classified operational data with non-cleared personnel (Level 3 or below without authorization) constitutes a Class-B Information Breach.
  • PROTOCOL 02: INTERNAL SURVEILLANCE
    All IA agents retain the right to audit the communications, quarters, and personal effects of any Site Aether personnel under suspicion of sedition. No warrant is required under Martial Law State 4.
  • PROTOCOL 03: ASSET DISPOSAL
    Field assets (informants, D-Class) compromising an operation must be neutralized immediately. The Foundation does not negotiate for the return of compromised intelligence assets.
  • PROTOCOL 04: MEMETIC HYGIENE
    Agents returning from AID assignments must undergo a 48-hour quarantine and cognito-hazard screening before accessing the Site Aether intranet.
